MapMaster makes learning world capitals genuinely fun. Learn one country, capital and a quick factual nugget each day, then put it to the test in a fast, 90-second daily quiz of ten random capitals. Score points, earn a rank, and watch your mastery grow.

HOW IT WORKS
- Learn: a fresh country and capital every day, with one true, bite-sized fact.
- Quiz: ten multiple-choice capitals against a 90-second clock.
- Rank: from Explorer to MapMaster based on your score.
- Streaks: keep your daily run alive.
